How it works

Screening Workflow

Our screening flow is built for simplicity: upload, process, and review. The entire pipeline happens locally on your machine for privacy and speed.

Start Analysis
1

Upload Image

Select a clear lesion image in JPG or PNG format. Ensure the lesion is centered and well-lit for the best screening results.

2

AI Preprocessing

The image is automatically normalized and resized. Our CNN pipeline prepares the data to ensure clinical compatibility with our model layers.

3

Model Prediction

The trained CNN analyzes the features and returns ranked probabilities across 7 distinct lesion classes, determining the most likely classification.

4

Review Report

A detailed report is generated instantly, showing the prediction, confidence level, and an AI attention map for visual confirmation.
